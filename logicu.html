LogICU

Version 1.93
An Android Logbook for Intensive Care Units
LogICU is a simple logbook designed for doctors training/working in Intensive Care Units (ICU) , which allows you to collect patient, support and procedural data on your mobile and to print the required reports/summaries. Features include:
  • Intuitive data entry, view, and search
  • Completed report/summary genration
  • Easy local and online backup
  • No need for Wi-Fi or 3G connection
