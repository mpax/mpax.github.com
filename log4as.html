Log4AS

Version 1.26
An Android Logbook for Anaesthetists/ Anesthesiologist
Log4AS is a simple logbook , which provids an intuitive user interface and key word suggestion for fast record entry. It can capature all the information required by the UK's RCOA and other anaesthetic professional bodies worldwide. Features include:
  • Intuitive data entry, view, and serach
  • Completed report/summary genration
  • Easy import from other system, such as iGasLog on iPhone
  • Easy local and online backup
