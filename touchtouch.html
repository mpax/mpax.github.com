TouchTouch: fun for infants

TouchTouch provides an interactive way for babies and newborns with visual and auditory stimulation. With TouchTouch, you can calm or amuse your baby by animated object and sound effect. Meanwhile, your baby can enjoy the touch fun.

TouchTouch will display and speak out the alphabets from A to Z, number from 0 to 9 to kick start babies early literacy. Animated objects and sound effects will entertain your kids with different colours while improving their hand eye co-ordination. Animated object and sound effect automatically changes for 8 seconds and can be triggered by a simple touch.
